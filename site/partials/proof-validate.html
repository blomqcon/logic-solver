Usage Notes:

  • All logic variables should be uppercase letters
  • When discharging assumptions is appropriate, simply do not list the assumptions in the assumptions column.
  • In the assumptions and justifications columns, list numbers in a comma separated list.
  • For MPP and MTT the conditional must be the first justification in the list.
  • For CP and RAA the assumption must be the first justification in the list.
  • For vE the first justification should the or statement, the second should be your first assumption, the third your first conclusion, the fourth your second assumption and the fifth your second conclusion.
